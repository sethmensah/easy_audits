Criteria

Standard Practices and payment procedures required that before payments are effected there should be memos to explain the activity and the purposes for which such payments should be made.

Condition

Payment vouchers amounting to GH¢{{ v }} were made by {{ data.facility_name | title }} to suppliers and individuals without memos to authenticate the transactions. Details in Appendix ‘J’.

Cause

This anomaly could be largely attributed mainly to the preparation of Payment Vouchers without recourse to any underlining document and deliberate disregard to financial rules.

Effect

The Payments could not be authenticated as there were no documents available for verification.

This practice could lead to duplicate payments as the supporting documents could be used as acquittals for different payment vouchers.

Recommendation

Management should produce the memos for audit scrutiny and authentication failure the amount be refunded by the approving, authorizing authority and necessary sanction preferred against them.
