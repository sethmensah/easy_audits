Criteria

As contained in the Ghana Gazette August, 2004 part VIII (Cash Payment No.14) where applicable, official receipt shall be obtained in support of payments to Departments, Enterprises and individuals, officers concerned in any stage of payments shall take steps to obtained receipts for the payment.

Condition

On the contrary, Payments Vouchers amounting to GH¢ {{ v }} made in respect of fuel purchased for workshops/Conferences/Meetings attended, Fuel for Official Vehicle, Genset and others as shown in Appendix ‘K’ did not have fuel receipts to authenticate the payment.

Cause

These lapses could be ascribed to laxity on the part of the accounts department to obtain fuel receipts from the beneficiaries to acquit the Payment Vouchers

Effect

The authenticity of such transaction could not be ascertained. Again, the practice is a violation of financial rule and discipline which could attract adverse audit observation.

Recommendation

Management should as a matter of urgency impress on the accounts department to obtain those receipts from the beneficiaries to authenticate the payments failure which the amount should be refunded by the beneficiaries.
