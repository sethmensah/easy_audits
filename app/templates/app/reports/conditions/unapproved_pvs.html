Criteria

Section D1: (Processing Payments, Statement of rules 3) of the ATF requires the Head of BMC to authorize Payment Vouchers before payments are made.

Condition

A total of (94) Payment Vouchers totaling GH¢{{v}} were not authorized by the Head of the facility or person assigned (Hospital Administrator) to authorize before payments were made. Details shown in Appendix ‘I’.

Cause

The lapse was due to non-compliance with financial regulation regarding the disbursement of public funds.

Effect

This practice if not checked could lead to payments being made for goods and services not provided, resulting in a loss of funds to the {{data.facility_name | title}}. Also, this practice casts doubt on the authenticity of the payments.

Recommendation

We recommended to management to have the payment vouchers appropriately certified and approved by the required officer of {{data.facility_name | title}} for our verification. Failing, the amount of GHc{{v}} should be surcharged to the Accountant.
