Criteria

FAR 2003, Section (43 and 44) states that payment shall be made only to the person or persons named on the payment voucher to their represented duly authorized in writing to receive the payment.

A public officer shall not on behalf of a department receive any payment from a member of the public expect as provided in the departmental accounting instruction, and failure to comply with this regulation is a bridge of financial discipline as defined in regulation 8(1).

Condition

On the contrary the Accounts Department, made payments to various suppliers and individuals but failed to ensure they append their signatures to acknowledge receipts of funds as payees on the Departmental Payment Vouchers as shown in Appendix ‘E’.

Cause

The accounts department did not see the need to let the suppliers and individuals sign to acknowledge receipt of funds.

Effect

Those transactions could not be authenticated and also we could not be assured whether those suppliers or individuals actually received those payments.

Recommendation

We recommend that all those payments are signed by the payees or their designated persons and the payment vouchers are produced for audit verification failure which the said amount should be refunded by the accounts department.
